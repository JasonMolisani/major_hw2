Positive Ramifications

Email

The internet opened new avenues for communication between people. Email is just one of the many new ways people can communicate. Instant messaging and video calls are just a few of the more basic options.

Wikipedia, Google, and More

The internet has led to more widespread access to knowledge. Wikipedia serves as a great way to get a basic understanding of a wide range of topics. If Wikipedia doesn't have the answer, Google is a search engine that can help find an answer. There are many other sources of information. Programmers can refer to Stack Overflow. People interesting in doing things themselves can look up tutorial videos on Youtube.

Content Creators

The presence of Youtube and the later emergence of Twitch led to the creation of a new type of job: content creator. It is now possible for a person to make a living from home by just making Youtube videos. There are other people who can make a living streaming themselves playing video games from their own room.

Ice Bucket Challenge

The internet also provides a great way to gather a large number of people for a cause. In 2014, the Ice Bucket Challenge was a viral campaign that raised over $115 dollars in 6 weeks to help find a cure for ALS. The premise was simple. Record a video of you having a bucket of cold water poured over your head. Make a donation. Challenge someone else.
